About Soupy Chef

Soupy Chef began as a small family kitchen in Lagos — rooted in recipes handed down through generations. Our mission is to make authentic Nigerian soups accessible, delicious, and convenient.

We work closely with local farmers for fresh ingredients and maintain traditional techniques to ensure that every bowl tastes like home.

Our Core Values

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional Nigerian cooking methods — slow simmering and balanced seasoning.

Freshness

We source produce daily from trusted markets to ensure quality and flavor.

Community

We support local farmers and celebrate Nigeria’s food culture by sharing it proudly with the world.
